Nigute nakwiyandikisha kuri TSINDA-DUTSINDE?

Niki igihe ntarengwa cyo kwinjira mu bizamini?

Igihe ntarengwa cyo kwinjira mu bizamini ni 24h mbere y'uko ibizamini bitangira. Turagira inama yo kwiyandikisha hakiri kare.

Nshobora kubona inkunga mu gihe mfite ikibazo?

Yego! Uramutse ufite ikibazo, tuzabafasha vuba. Ushobora kutwandikira kuri email cyangwa kutwandikira kuri WhatsApp.

Gukora iki niba habaye ikibazo kidashobora gukemurwa?

Mu gihe ikibazo kidashobora gukemurwa byihuse, tuzakurikirana ikibazo cyawe tugufashe kugikemura mu buryo bwihuse.
