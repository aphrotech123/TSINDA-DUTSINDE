Ibibazo Bikunze Kubazwa (FAQs)

Turashaka ko wumva neza serivisi zacu! Muri iyi ngingo, uzasangamo ibisubizo by'ibibazo bikunze kubazwa na benshi, tukaba twizera ko bizagufasha kubona amakuru wifuza mu buryo bwihuse kandi bworoshye.

Nigute nakwiyandikisha kuri TSINDA-DUTSINDE?

Kugira ngo wiyandikishe, hitamo "Register" kuri urubuga rwacu, ukoreho kuri form yawe, wemeze amakuru yawe kandi uhitemo ijambo ry’ibanga.

Niki gihe ntarengwa cyo kwinjira mu bizamini?

Igihe ntarengwa cyo kwinjira mu bizamini ni 24h mbere y'uko ibizamini bitangira. Turagira inama yo kwiyandikisha hakiri kare.

Nshobora kubona inkunga mu gihe mfite ikibazo?

Yego! Uramutse ufite ikibazo, tuzabafasha vuba. Ushobora kutwandikira kuri email cyangwa kutwandikira kuri WhatsApp.

Nigute nshobora kwiyandikisha mu kizamini?

Iyo umaze kwiyandikisha, uzajya uhabwa amahirwe yo kwiyandikisha mu kizamini ushyiraho igihe cyabyo.

Nshobora kwiga ibijyanye n'ibizamini imbere mu gihe gito?

Yego, dufite uburyo bwo gukoresha ibisubizo by'ibizamini byashize ndetse n'amakuru ajyanye n'ibyiza mu by'ubumenyi.

Ubushobozi bwo kumenya ibizami bitandukanye burashobora kugabanywa?

Yego, dufite uburyo bwo kwigisha ibizamini bitandukanye kandi dufite gahunda zigamije kubafasha kwiyungura ubushobozi.

Nkora iki mu gihe ntabonye igisubizo ku kibazo cyanjye?

Twemera ko ibibazo byose bisubizwa vuba. Wabona igisubizo cyihuse, cyangwa watura ku itsinda ryacu ritanga inkunga.

Ni iki kizanabasha gutunganya igihe cy’ibizamini?

Ibizamini bitunganywa hakurikijwe gahunda y'ikizamini ya TSINDA-DUTSINDE.

Niki nakora igihe nageze mu kizamini?

Mugihe ugeze mu kizamini, fata umwanya wo gusoma neza amategeko n’amabwiriza ajyanye n’ibizami.

Ese nshobora gukora ikizamini imbere mu gihe cyateganyijwe?

Ntabwo byemewe, ibizamini bikozwe gusa ku munsi n’igihe cyateganyijwe.

Ngiyehe kubona ibisubizo by'ibizamini?

Ibisubizo by'ibizamini bizajya bihabwa abayobozi ku rubuga rwacu kandi bizatangwa ku gihe cyateganyijwe.

Nzi gute ko nabonye igisubizo cyiza?

Ubwiza bw'ibisubizo by'ibizamini bizemezwa hakurikijwe amanota yawe no kugenzura imikorere yawe mu kizamini.

Nigute nakora igihe mbonye ikibazo n'ibisubizo?

Kuri buri kibazo cyawe, turagusaba kutwandikira tukaba twagusubiza.

Ese hari igihe tutemerewe gukora ibizamini?

Yego, bizamini bisubirwamo gusa ku gihe cyateganyijwe.

Ese ikizamini kiriho umutekano w’ibikorwa byanjye?

Yego, turagufasha gucunga ibizamini kandi turinda umutekano w'amakuru yawe.

Nzi gute ko uburyo bw'ibizamini bukoreshwa butandukanye n'ubusanzwe?

Ibizamini byacu byateguwe neza kandi bizaba mu buryo budasanzwe.

Nshobora kubona ibizamini byose mu rurimi rw'ikinyarwanda?

Yego, ibisubizo by'ibizamini bizatangwa mu rurimi rw'ikinyarwanda.

Nshobora gukora ibizamini ku buryo bworoshye?

Yego, dufite uburyo bworoshye bwo gukora ibizamini ku rubuga rwacu.

Ese nsigara ku bikorwa byanjye byose kuri TSINDA-DUTSINDE?

Yego, uzahora ubonera ibikorwa byose kuri dashboard yawe.

Nshobora kubona ibisubizo byanjye vuba?

Yego, ibizami bizagenda bitangwa mu gihe cyateganyijwe kandi uzahora ubona ibisubizo.
